Tribute to Maham

A bright student from Luton who loves traveling and exploring the world!

About Maham

Maham is a 15-year-old student studying in Year 9. She is exceptionally bright in her studies and has a passion for traveling. She has visited several countries, including France, Pakistan, and Egypt, and loves exploring historical places.

Historical Places Maham Has Explored

France

Maham visited the iconic Eiffel Tower in Paris and the Palace of Versailles, a symbol of French royalty and history.

Pakistan

In Pakistan, Maham explored the majestic Badshahi Mosque in Lahore and the ancient ruins of Mohenjo-Daro, a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Egypt

During her trip to Egypt, Maham marveled at the Great Pyramids of Giza and the Sphinx, symbols of ancient Egyptian civilization.

Dream Destinations

Maham dreams of visiting Italy to see the Colosseum and Venice, and Japan to experience the cherry blossoms and Mount Fuji.
